Profile

Machine Learning Engineer who owns GenAI systems end to end — from research and experimentation through the production deployment that real users depend on.

I specialise in large language models, transformers and gradient-boosted models for structured information extraction from unstructured financial text. At Accenture I build enterprise GenAI for financial-services and pharmaceutical clients: real-time NLP pipelines, RAG over dense vector databases, and the evaluation and annotation tooling that gates every release — LLM-as-judge scoring, error-taxonomy analysis and hallucination tracking.

My research foundation comes from an ML internship at IIT Kharagpur applying computer vision to a wildlife-conservation programme. I work closely with domain experts, PMs and business stakeholders, and I'm good at turning model failure modes into measurable gains in production reliability. That's the forward-deployed part — I go where the problem is.
